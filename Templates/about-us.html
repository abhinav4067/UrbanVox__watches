Our Story

UrbanVox, a trailblazing watch company, emerged in 2010, founded by visionary watchmaker Alex Urban. Urban's passion for horology ignited during his youth, fostering a dream of blending timeless craftsmanship with contemporary urban flair. In its nascent years, UrbanVox faced skepticism but swiftly captivated enthusiasts with avant-garde designs. Urban's commitment to innovation propelled the company, pioneering bold materials and unconventional mechanisms. By 2015, UrbanVox gained recognition for its fusion of precision engineering and urban aesthetics. Collaborations with renowned artists and designers propelled its reputation globally. In 2018, UrbanVox introduced its signature "Cityscape" collection, reflecting metropolitan skylines in intricate dials. This series resonated with urbanites seeking sophistication intertwined with metropolitan vibes. In 2020, UrbanVox unveiled its breakthrough "EcoTech" line, integrating sustainable materials and eco-conscious manufacturing processes. This initiative resonated with environmentally-conscious consumers, expanding UrbanVox's reach. By 2022, UrbanVox stood as a paradigm of contemporary watchmaking, blending tradition with innovation. Its commitment to quality, design, and sustainability ensured its enduring legacy in the horological landscape.
